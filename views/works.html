Works of Linkin Park

Albums

  • Hybrid Theory (2000): Debut album featuring hits like "In the End" and "Crawling."
  • Meteora (2003): A follow-up album with successful tracks such as "Numb" and "Somewhere I Belong."
  • Minutes to Midnight (2007): Marked a shift in style with singles like "What I've Done" and "Bleed It Out."

Singles

  • In the End (2001): Iconic song known for its powerful lyrics and memorable chorus.
  • Numb (2003): Emotional single that became one of Linkin Park's signature songs.
  • What I've Done (2007): Featured in the film Transformers, addressing themes of redemption.

Achievements

Linkin Park has achieved global success with numerous awards, including Grammy Awards. They are recognized for their blend of rock and electronic elements, and their impact on the alternative music scene.
